Our Mission

FarmersPulse started with a simple idea: to help small-scale Kenyan farmers access reliable, personalized information that makes their work easier and more productive. We use AI to match advisories, market prices, weather alerts, and pest warnings to each farmer's specific location, crops, and context — so every notification you receive is relevant to your farm.

How the AI Personalization Works

When you set up your profile — your county, your crops, your farm size — our AI model reads that context and filters the information feed specifically for you. A maize farmer in Narok will see different advisories than a tea farmer in Nyeri. The more complete your profile, the better we can serve you.
